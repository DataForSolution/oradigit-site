OraDigit Workplace Policy

OraDigit is committed to creating a flexible, inclusive, and high-performance environment that supports individual growth, team collaboration, and ethical innovation. Our workplace policy reflects our values of trust, responsibility, and empowermentâ€”aligned with our mission to build human-centered AI solutions.

Workplace Structure

  • Remote-First: We offer a remote-first structure that supports global collaboration with the flexibility to work from anywhere.
  • Flexible Hours: We believe in asynchronous communication and outcome-based work rather than rigid schedules.
  • Clear Accountability: Roles, goals, and expectations are transparent and collaboratively managed.

Inclusion & Equity

  • We are committed to inclusive hiring, supporting underrepresented talent in AI and tech.
  • Diverse perspectives are actively welcomed in team decisions, project design, and product outcomes.

Well-Being & Growth

  • We support mental wellness through work-life balance, optional mental health days, and regular check-ins.
  • Continuous learning is encouraged via internal workshops, mentoring, and exploration through OraDigit Labs.

Ethical Collaboration

  • We hold ourselves to high standards of integrity, transparency, and fairness in every collaboration.
  • Responsible AI and privacy-first data handling are foundational in all client and internal work.
