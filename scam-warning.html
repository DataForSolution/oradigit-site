Scam Warning

Effective Date: June 2025

OraDigit has been made aware of fraudulent emails, social media messages, or unauthorized individuals falsely claiming to represent OraDigit in job offers, business partnerships, or project funding.

Common Warning Signs

  • Unsolicited messages claiming to be from “OraDigit HR” or “OraDigit Investments”
  • Requests for payment, bank information, or gift cards
  • Job offers that do not go through our official site or channels
  • Suspicious domains like @oradigit-careers.com or @oradigit-support.net

Protect Yourself

  • Never share personal or financial information with unknown senders
  • Verify email addresses—official messages only come from @oradigit.com
  • Report suspicious messages to admin@oradigit.com

Official OraDigit Communication

All legitimate communication from OraDigit will come from verified channels, such as:

  • This website: www.oradigit.com
  • Official email: admin@oradigit.com
  • LinkedIn: OraDigit on LinkedIn

Contact Us

If you suspect fraud or impersonation, contact us immediately at admin@oradigit.com.
