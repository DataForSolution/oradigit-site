Frequently Asked Questions

What does OraDigit specialize in?

OraDigit LLC is a professional services firm focused on applied artificial intelligence and advanced data solutions. We help organizations leverage AI to make smarter decisions, automate processes, and uncover actionable insights—from prototyping to production deployment.

Who are OraDigit’s typical clients?

We work with mid-size to enterprise organizations across sectors including finance, healthcare, education, energy, government, and retail. Whether you’re building your first machine learning model or scaling a data platform, we support you every step of the way.

What services do you offer?

  • AI Strategy & Roadmapping
  • Custom Machine Learning Models (NLP, CV, LLMs)
  • Data Engineering & Cloud Infrastructure
  • Predictive Analytics & BI Dashboards
  • MLOps, Deployment, & Automation

What makes OraDigit different?

Our edge is hybrid intelligence—the fusion of AI capabilities with domain expertise and human insight. We don’t just build models; we embed intelligence into operations, develop sustainable solutions, and partner closely with clients to ensure impact.

Is OraDigit a product company or consultancy?

OraDigit is a service-oriented consultancy that also develops reusable AI frameworks and internal toolkits. We prioritize flexibility and tailor each engagement to the client’s infrastructure and data maturity.

Do you offer live demos or proofs of concept?

Yes. We offer interactive proof-of-concept models, Streamlit/Gradio dashboards, and sandbox environments depending on project scope. These are helpful for stakeholder buy-in and technical validation.

Can OraDigit help us scale from pilot to production?

Absolutely. We support full AI lifecycle: model development, API integration, deployment pipelines, monitoring, and governance. We also help teams adopt MLOps best practices using tools like MLflow, Docker, and CI/CD workflows.

Where is OraDigit based?

OraDigit LLC is based in Lanham, Maryland, USA. We operate with a distributed team and work with clients both locally and globally.

How can I start working with OraDigit?

Contact us through the Contact Us page or email admin@oradigit.com. We'll follow up with a discovery call to understand your needs and map out how we can collaborate.
