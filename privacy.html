Privacy Policy

Effective Date: June 2025

OraDigit is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you visit our website or interact with our services.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect personal information you provide voluntarily (e.g., via contact forms or email) including your name, email address, organization, and any additional information you submit.

We may also collect technical data (e.g., IP address, browser type, location, cookies) through analytics or embedded services.

2. How We Use Information

We use your information to:

  • Respond to your inquiries
  • Provide access to services or demos
  • Improve website experience and content
  • Ensure website security and prevent abuse

3. Cookies

We use cookies for analytics, user preferences, and site performance. You may control cookie settings via your browser or our cookie banner. For more, see our Cookie Policy.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell your data. We may share it with trusted partners (e.g., hosting providers or analytics platforms) under strict confidentiality agreements.

5. Data Storage

Your data may be stored in the United States or other jurisdictions. We ensure data is handled according to applicable privacy laws (e.g., GDPR, CCPA).

6. Your Rights

You may request access, correction, or deletion of your data at any time by contacting us at admin@oradigit.com.

7. Updates to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Changes will be posted here with an updated effective date.

8. Contact

If you have questions or concerns, contact us at admin@oradigit.com.
