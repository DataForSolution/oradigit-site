Cookie Policy

Effective Date: June 2025

This Cookie Policy explains how OraDigit uses cookies and similar technologies to recognize you when you visit our website at www.oradigit.com. It explains what these technologies are, why we use them, and your rights to control our use of them.

What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small data files placed on your computer or mobile device when you visit a website. Cookies are widely used to make websites work, improve their functionality, and provide reporting information.

Why We Use Cookies

  • Essential Cookies: Required to enable basic site functionality.
  • Analytics Cookies: Help us understand how visitors interact with the site (e.g., pages visited, time on site).
  • Preference Cookies: Store your preferences, such as language or region.
  • Marketing Cookies: (Currently unused) In the future, may be used to deliver targeted ads.

How to Control Cookies

You can accept or reject non-essential cookies via our cookie banner. Additionally, you can control or delete cookies via your browser settings.

Third-Party Cookies

Some third-party services (e.g., analytics platforms or embedded content providers) may use cookies. Their use is governed by their own policies.

Updates

We may update this Cookie Policy periodically. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date.

Contact Us
