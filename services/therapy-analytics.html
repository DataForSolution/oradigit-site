Therapy Analytics & Predictive Modeling

OraDigit helps healthcare providers harness AI to predict treatment outcomes, stratify risk, and optimize therapy workflows—especially in cancer care, nuclear medicine, and radiology-driven interventions.

Empowering Treatment Decisions with Data

Using AI and advanced analytics, we enable personalized treatment planning, monitor therapy progress, and model potential outcomes—helping clinicians deliver care that’s smarter, safer, and more effective.

  • Response Prediction Models
    Train AI models to forecast patient response to radiopharmaceutical therapy, immunotherapy, or chemoradiation using historical imaging and lab data.
  • Treatment Pathway Optimization
    Analyze multimodal datasets to recommend optimal therapy routes, timelines, and combinations for specific cancer profiles.
  • AI for Dosimetry Planning
    Use AI-enhanced image analysis to support radiation dose estimation, toxicity risk modeling, and therapy planning for nuclear medicine treatments.
  • Progression Risk Modeling
    Forecast likelihood of recurrence, metastasis, or therapy resistance through statistical and deep learning approaches.
  • Outcome Dashboarding
    Interactive dashboards for oncologists, radiologists, and administrators to track key performance and therapy metrics in real time.
