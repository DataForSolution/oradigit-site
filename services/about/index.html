About OraDigit

OraDigit is a healthcare AI company built by experts in PET/CT, radiology, and data science. Our goal is to deliver trustworthy, impactful solutions for medical imaging, diagnostics, and therapy optimization.

Our Mission

To bridge clinical expertise and cutting-edge AI technology to transform radiology and nuclear medicine—making imaging workflows faster, more accurate, and insight-driven.

Who We Are

  • Healthcare Professionals
    With over 15 years in PET/CT and nuclear medicine, we understand the practical and clinical challenges first-hand.
  • AI Engineers & Data Scientists
    We design models and tools that solve high-impact imaging problems using responsible, explainable AI.
  • Clinical Collaborators
    We work directly with hospitals, research institutions, and therapy centers to build real-world-ready solutions.

What Sets Us Apart

  • Imaging-Focused AI
    We specialize in AI models for radiology, therapy planning, and PET/CT segmentation.
  • Domain Expertise
    Our approach is deeply informed by clinical workflows, regulatory needs, and operational challenges.
  • Built on Trust
    Transparency, safety, and long-term value are core to how we build and deliver AI.
