Clinical Decision Support Systems

OraDigit enables radiologists, oncologists, and nuclear medicine teams to make faster, more accurate decisions by combining real-time dashboards with explainable AI. Our tools help clinicians interpret imaging, track metrics, and act confidently at the point of care.

How We Support Clinical Decision-Making

  • Real-Time Dashboards
    Aggregate imaging data, lab results, and AI predictions into intuitive interfaces for physicians.
  • AI-Driven Flagging Systems
    Automatically identify suspicious findings or outliers using predictive models trained on PET/CT and radiology datasets.
  • Explainable AI Visuals
    Deliver saliency maps, confidence scores, and heatmaps to help clinicians understand model outputs.
  • Workflow Integration
    Seamless embedding into PACS, RIS, or hospital dashboards using secure APIs or HL7/FHIR standards.
  • Alerting & Escalation Logic
    Notify care teams when urgent, abnormal, or unexpected values are detected in imaging or data streams.

Who Benefits

  • Radiology Departments
    Reduce diagnostic variability and improve confidence with assisted interpretation tools.
  • Oncology & Nuclear Medicine Units
    Monitor therapeutic progress, compare response over time, and receive intelligent alerts.
  • Hospital Administrators
    Track performance metrics and streamline imaging workflow using real-time analytics.
  • Clinical Researchers
    Visualize and audit AI predictions and imaging annotations for reproducibility and trust.
