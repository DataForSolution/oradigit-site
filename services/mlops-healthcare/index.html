AI Strategy & MLOps for Healthcare

OraDigit enables healthcare teams to deploy, monitor, and scale AI responsibly. From imaging models to therapy prediction systems, we ensure your AI infrastructure is compliant, explainable, and built to last.

AI Built for Clinical Reality

  • Monitoring & Drift Detection

    Maintain version control, model lineage, and approval logs to meet regulatory and institutional requirements.

    Build reproducible environments with isolated test beds and CI/CD for AI workflows.
  • Human-in-the-Loop (HITL)
    Enable expert review, override, and validation feedback in the AI lifecycle — vital for high-stakes medical applications.
