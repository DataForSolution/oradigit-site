Healthcare Data Platforms & Imaging Infrastructure

OraDigit designs and implements scalable data platforms tailored to healthcare imaging. We help hospitals, research teams, and radiology networks build secure DICOM repositories, real-time data pipelines, and interoperable analytics environments.

What We Deliver

  • DICOM Data Infrastructure
    Structured storage, indexing, and retrieval of medical imaging across PACS and research environments.
  • Secure Cloud Pipelines
    Stream data from scanners to the cloud using HIPAA-compliant transport, encryption, and access control.
  • Metadata Normalization
    Clean, harmonize, and enrich imaging metadata for AI training, clinical dashboards, and cross-center comparison.
  • Structured Research Repositories
    Build clean, queryable image datasets for clinical trials, research studies, and AI development workflows.
  • FHIR & HL7 Integration
    Integrate imaging with EMR and clinical data systems using healthcare interoperability standards.

Use Cases

  • Hospitals & Imaging Networks
    Centralize and secure imaging data across modalities, sites, and departments.
  • Academic Research Labs
    Prepare clean, de-identified image datasets for reproducible science and publication.
  • AI Model Training & Evaluation
    Enable robust model development with structured, versioned imaging pipelines.
  • Regulatory Compliance & Audits
    Maintain data lineage, access logs, and backup strategies for institutional and legal requirements.
