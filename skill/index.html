FAQs

What’s the difference between Whole Body and Skull-to-Thigh PET/CT?

“Whole Body” includes head and extremities; most oncology protocols use Skull-to-Thigh unless payer/indication requires otherwise.

Can OraDigit embed in our EHR?

Yes. We can present order guidance as a link-out or an embedded web view; details vary by EHR.

Do you provide payer-specific policy phrasing?

Yes. We maintain lightweight, updateable modules that mirror payer language to reduce avoidable denials.
